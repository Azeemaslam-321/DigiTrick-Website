Automation FAQ

Will chatbot replace my team?

No. It handles first-level questions and lead capture. Important conversations should still move to your team.

Can it work with website enquiries?

Yes. It can work alongside forms and CTAs to capture cleaner enquiry details and route them faster.

Can we add automation later?

Yes. It can be added after website, app or software launch once common questions and workflows are clear.

Is this useful for small businesses?

Yes, especially for businesses receiving repeated queries about pricing, services, timings, admission or appointment availability.
