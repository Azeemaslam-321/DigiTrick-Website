Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about our services, training, and solutions.

Q1. What services does Digi Trick Consultancy provide?

Digi Trick Consultancy offers a wide range of services including Corporate & College Training, Job Support, Digital Marketing, E-commerce Setup, IT Development (Web, Mobile, UI/UX, Cloud Solutions, Software Development), and Employment Services (C2C & W2).

Q2. Do you provide online as well as offline training?

Yes, we offer both online and offline training programs. Our flexible delivery models are designed to meet the needs of individual learners as well as corporate clients.

Q3. Who can join your training programs?

Our training programs are open to students, freshers, working professionals, and corporate teams. We design our content to suit different experience levels and learning goals.

Q4. Do you provide certifications after training?

Yes, participants receive a certificate of completion after successfully finishing the training. This certification helps enhance career opportunities and validates acquired skills.

Q5. Can businesses hire Digi Trick Consultancy for development projects?

Yes, we provide end-to-end development services including Website Development, Mobile App Development, E-commerce Setup, and Custom Software Solutions. Our team works closely with clients to understand their requirements and deliver innovative, scalable, and cost-effective solutions.

Q6. Do you offer corporate training programs?

Absolutely. We specialize in delivering corporate training programs tailored to upskill employees in the latest technologies and tools. Our corporate training ensures practical knowledge transfer and helps organizations enhance productivity, innovation, and workforce efficiency.

Q7. How do I enroll in a training or internship program?

Enrolling is simple. You can contact us through our website, LinkedIn page, or official email. Our team will share program details, schedules, and registration steps, ensuring a smooth onboarding process.

Q8. What makes Digi Trick Consultancy different from others?

Our unique approach lies in combining practical, hands-on training with real-time projects, along with offering complete IT solutions under one roof. We focus equally on individual learners, corporate clients, and businesses, ensuring value-driven results for all.

Q9. Do you provide job support services?

Yes, we assist working professionals with project-specific job support in various technologies. Our expert trainers provide guidance on real-time issues, coding, and project execution, helping professionals perform better in their roles.
